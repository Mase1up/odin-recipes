Soft boiled eggs

Yummy yummy in my tummy

Soft-boiled eggs go great in so many dishes. Salads, ramen, sandwiches, and much more.


Ingredients

  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Place eggs in water and boil for 7 minutes
  3. Remove eggs from water and place in an ice bath
  4. After around five minutes, peel eggs underwater or under running water
